=========================================
YASU'S BACKROOMS MATERIAL PACK
=========================================

Author: @mvyasu
License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)

-----------------------------------------

A pack of PBR materials inspired by The Backrooms, including wallpaper, ceiling tiles, carpets, and lights. I decided to create these and share them because I couldn't find an existing pack.

Every material comes with a regular colored version and a black-and-white version so you can easily recolor them to match your project's vibe.

-----------------------------------------

I made a similar material pack a few years back, but I knew the ceiling materials I made wouldn't work with a high resolution so I did some research on potential options.
